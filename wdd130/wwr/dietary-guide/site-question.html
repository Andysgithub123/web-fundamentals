Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of the dietary planner?

The purpose of the dietary planner is to yield results based on what you are looking for, whether that be to bulk up, lose weight, or anything in between.

Why am I not seeing results?

Everyone's body is unique there is multiple reasons why you might not see initial results for example keto is a low carb diet and works for most people because generally we consume a lot of carbs however if you are someone who regularly does not have a lot of carbs in your diet there is not going to be much change.

Why am I not gaining weight even though I am eating more?

There are several things that impact a person's metabolic rate but we can classify it into two categories. The first is genetic, some people are just born with faster or slower metabolic rates which can play a role but is not the end all be all. The second category is outside factors such as how active you are in your day to day life, how often you workout along with what kind of workouts you are doing, and your emotional well being.
